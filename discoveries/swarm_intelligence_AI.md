# Agent Discovery: swarm intelligence AI

## Search Query
Swarm intelligence artificial intelligence repository

## Summary
Here are 5 bullet points summarizing the GitHub repositories for Overse Labs:

* The SASI@home project (cgbarlow/sasi) explores the concept of distributed AI development through swarm intelligence, where multiple agents collaborate to advance towards Artificial Super Intelligence, inspired by the SETI@home project.
* The InterPlanetary Artificial Intelligence (IPAI) project (humandataincome/ipai) enables users to store and manage their personal AI models in a censorship-resistant manner, leveraging the InterPlanetary Data Wallet (IPDW) for decentralized and private AI research.
* The BoidFlow simulation (phassic/BoidFlow) demonstrates principles of swarm intelligence and artificial life by modeling the collective movement of agents (boids) to mimic natural behaviors such as flocking, schooling, or herding.
* The Artificial-Intelligence--Francisco repository (12shiela/Artificial-Intelligence--Francisco) provides Python implementations of key AI algorithms, including Genetic Algorithm (GA), Particle Swarm Optimization (PSO), and Markov Decision Process (MDP), as part of a homework assignment for an AI course.
* The PSO-AI-2019 repository (casted0/PSO-AI-2019) is a final project for an Artificial Intelligence course, focusing on the implementation of the Particle Swarm Optimization (PSO) algorithm, a popular optimization technique used in AI and machine learning applications.
